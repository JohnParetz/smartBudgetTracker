About Smart Budget Tracker

Learn more about the purpose of the app

This app does:

Smart Budget Tracker helps users track income and expenses in a simple and organized way. It calculates the totals automatically and shows the remaining balance.

Why it was created:

The purpose of this app is to help users better understand their financial habits and make budgeting easier with a clean and simple design.
